Final detail page

My kite is leading-edge down

Most likely cause

The kite crashed in a standard position and needs a normal relaunch.

Also check

  • Sufficient wind
  • Lines are clear
  • Kite is inflated properly

What to do

  1. Release bar pressure.
  2. Pull one steering line gradually.
  3. Allow the kite to rotate toward a wingtip.
  4. Complete the relaunch normally.

Trainer tip

Patience usually works better than force during relaunch.

What success looks like

The kite rotates onto a wingtip and relaunches.

App highlights

Made for learners, instructors, and post-session review.

Problem-first navigation

Start from the thing that went wrong, then narrow into the most likely cause and what to check next.

Instructor-style guidance

Each entry uses concise fixes, trainer tips, and success cues that are easy to scan between supervised attempts.

Works as a quick reference

No accounts, feeds, or distractions. Just structured guidance for launch, relaunch, water starts, transitions, upwind riding, and rescue.

Safety note

A reference, not a replacement for instruction.

Kite Coach is educational support for learning conversations, beach briefings, and post-session review. It does not replace professional instruction, local rules, weather judgment, equipment checks, or supervised safety training.
