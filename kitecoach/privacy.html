Privacy Policy

Kite Coach does not collect user data.

Effective date: June 16, 2026

App Store privacy summary: Kite Coach does not collect, store, sell, share, track, or transmit any user data.

Data Collection

Kite Coach does not collect personal information, contact information, location data, identifiers, usage data, diagnostics, financial information, health information, fitness information, browsing history, search history, purchases, user content, or any other data from users.

Tracking

Kite Coach does not track users across apps or websites owned by other companies. The app does not use advertising identifiers, analytics trackers, third-party advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

Accounts and Sign-In

Kite Coach does not require an account, login, email address, phone number, or profile. The app is designed as an offline educational reference.

Third Parties

Kite Coach does not share user data with third parties because no user data is collected by the app.

Children's Privacy

Kite Coach does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If Kite Coach's data practices change in the future, this privacy policy will be updated before any data collection begins.

Contact

For privacy questions about Kite Coach, contact the developer through the support channel listed on the App Store.
