FAQ

Common Questions

General

What account do I use?

Use the same Tempest account you use for Tempest lifestyle apps.

Do I need a Tempest account?

Yes. A free Tempest account is enough to sign in. Your iWind subscription unlocks iWind features.

Sign up for a free Tempest account at Tempest LIfestyle Apps.

What does the subscription include?

Your subscription includes access to Tempest network spots, favorite profiles, forecast models, radar, and more.

Some Tempest spots are only available to upgraded (paid) Tempest accounts. That is a separate subscription.

iPhone

How do I add a profile?

Open Profiles, tap the add button, name the profile, then save it.

How do I add a spot?

Open a profile, tap the add button in Spots, search for a location, select a result, then add it to the profile.

How do I change units?

Open Settings and choose your distance, wind, and temperature units.

How do I see Getting Started again?

Open Help and choose Getting Started. You can also start the phone or watch tour from Help.

Watch

How do I find charts and radar?

See How To

How do I find radar?

Open radar from the spot view by swiping to the right.

How do I find forecast charts?

Open forecast charts by clicking on a spot. Some spots come with multiple charts so you can compare available forecast models. If more than one chart you can scroll to see the others.

Why does watch data look stale?

There are multiple reasons this can happen.

The most common reason this happens is due to the spot not being updated recently. The weather station data update timelines vary by location and equipment. Some spots update every 5 minutes while others update once a day. It is also possible that the spot is completely offline but not reporting such.

Ensure your device is connected to the internet.

Ensure you are signed in to the app on the phone and your phone and watch are nearby long enough to sync.
