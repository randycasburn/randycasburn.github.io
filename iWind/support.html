Support

Please take the time to describe the issue as precisely as you can.

Screenshots are really helpful too.

Two ways to get support:

  1. Send an e-mail to: support@iwind.atlassian.net
  2. Navigate to the support web site and click the button to [Report an Issue].

Sending an e-mail will ensure I can respond directly back to you. The form is anonymous and I won't be able to respond directly.

Where's Android?

If you want an Android version of iWind, please contact me via the support methods above.

If there is sufficient interest, I'll consider it for future development. I'll need at least 10 of you who are willing to pay for the subscription to make it happen.

So gather all your friends around and pitch the idea!
