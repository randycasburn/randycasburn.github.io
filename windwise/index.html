Knowledge-centric, not data-centric

WindWise understands the rider, the sport, and the spot.

Identical weather can produce different recommendations for different riders. WindWise combines Sport Knowledge, Rider Knowledge, Spot Knowledge, and Weather Knowledge to make a recommendation that fits the session, not just the forecast.

Rider Knowledge

Skill, goals, equipment, preferences, risk tolerance, and personal comfort.

Sport Knowledge

Wind ranges, gust tolerance, water state, session duration, and comfort thresholds.

Spot Knowledge

Preferred directions, launch behavior, local effects, sensor trust, and shared history.

Weather Knowledge

Current conditions, model trends, storm signals, daylight, and changing context.

The weather is only an input. The output is a decision.
