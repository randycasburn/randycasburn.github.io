★ Windspeed Alert

Set a wind speed in Settings and the app will alert you when your selected spot hits or exceeds that setting. The alert consists of a simple ping and haptic vibration.

★ Work Out Session

When you enable Water Lock, a workout session will automatically start. This captures and records information that is added to your workout sessions on your watch.
