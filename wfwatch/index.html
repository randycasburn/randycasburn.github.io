Features

Favorites Profiles

Choose from one of your pre-configured Weatherflow-Tempest Favorite's Profiles

What are Favorites Profiles?

View Spots From Selected Profile

The main screen presents a scrollable list of spots. The view includes the spot name, wind speed, direction and the last update time.

Hourly Chart

Displays line chart with past two hours history and next hour of wind prediction

Weekly Chart

Displays bar chart with next seven days of wind prediction

Map with Animated Weather Radar

Displays map with spot location and animated weather radar for previous few hours and next 30 minutes

Water Lock

Place watch in WaterLock mode to prevent screen interaction while on the water

Complications/Widgets

Complications can be placed onto any watchface. Tap any widget to open the app. Corner & Circular widgets show wind direction (arrow) and wind speed in text . Rectangular widgets show line chart and text with spot name and current speed

Last Spot Viewed

  1. When you open the app it will automatically show the previous spot your were viewing
  2. When you change the spot in view all the widgets will now automatically reflect data for that spot
