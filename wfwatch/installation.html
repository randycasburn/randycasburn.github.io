Installation

NOTE: This is a Watch Only app - it is unlike other iPhone/Watch apps you've installed before.

You can install the app from your watch or from your iPhone.


Follow these steps to install the app from your watch

  1. Find the link in a message or e-mail on your watch
  2. The provided link will open the product page on the App Store
    • The product is hidden/not discoverable on the App Store
    • Do not share the provided link with anyone else
  3. Follow the prompts to aquire and download the app
  4. Press the [Open] button when presented
  5. When the app launches, follow the "First Launch" instructions provided

Follow these steps to install the app from your iPhone

  1. Find the link in a message or e-mail on your iPhone
  2. The provided link will open the product page on the App Store
    • The product is hidden/not discoverable on the App Store
    • Do not share the provided link with anyone else
  3. Follow the prompts to aquire and download the app
    • The app will not show up in the list of iPhone apps
    • This is what makes it different from other apps
    • The app is now installed on your watch
  4. On your watch, tap the crown (scroll wheel) one time
  5. Find the app icon that looks like a yellow arrow and tap it
  6. When the app launches, follow the "First Launch" instructions provided

Follow these steps to remove and reinstall the app

  1. REMOVE:
    1. Open the Watch app on your iPhone
    2. Scroll to "INSTALLED ON APPLE WATCH" section
    3. Click on "WF Favorites Watch"
    4. Click on "Delete app on Apple Watch"
    5. Click "delete" in the popup
  2. RE-INSTALL:
    1. On your watch, tap the crown (scroll wheel) one time
    2. Find the app store icon and tap it
    3. Scroll all the way to the bottom and tap "Account"
    4. Tap "Purchased"
    5. Tap "My Purchases"
    6. Find "WF Favorites Watch" in the list
    7. Tap the download cloud icon
    8. Press the [Open] button when presented
    9. When the app launches, follow the "First Launch" instructions provided
